Technology

Technology that drives payments

IFTAS is at the heart of a fast-moving payment revolution. Our state-of-the-art Infrastructure powers payments in India and the globe. We meet the rapidly evolving needs of hundreds of banks and billions of consumers – all eager to benefit from new and future payment methods.

Know More

Gift to Bhutan

IFTAS launched its innovative product - Global Interchange for Financial Transaction (GIFT) - in Bhutan, to promote cashless payments and bolster the growth of the financial sector of the country. The product, with its seamless and ease of use, promotes operational efficiency and helps people transact easily. GIFT was launched in Bhutan on 22nd July 2019.
