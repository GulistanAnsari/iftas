ABOUT US

OVERVIEW

A wholly owned subsidiary of the Reserve Bank of India, IFTAS, provides essential IT related services to RBI, banks, and financial institutions. It also provides various solutions that ensure a seamless and smooth experience for consumers. IFTAS’ flagship products and services have been recognized as ‘critical infrastructure of the country’, because of their ability to solve complex problems, provide simple and effective solutions, and providing an innovative and unique digital banking experience. The unique products offered by IFTAS are designed to solve various banking and payment related complexities while providing a simple and secure experience to both, the bank as well as the consumers.

IFTAS’ innovative products like INFINET and SFMS enable Inter-bank/GST/ governments’ payment and receipt transactions to the Reserve Bank, other banks, and co-operative societies. The Indian Banking Community Cloud, on the other hand, provides cloud-based solutions like mobile banking, financial inclusion business correspondent, etc. to enable digitisation of banks.

Apart from offering several unique products, IFTAS also offers consulting services to banks central banks of countries, across functions like technology, governance, process, tools, skills, etc. to help them improve their offerings.

All the products and services offered by IFTAS are aimed to provide uninterrupted banking services to the nation, round the clock.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted technology organization that delivers business excellence in products and services to central banks and banking and financial industry, globally.

Our Mission

Powered by our expertise and driven by the thought of digitization, our mission is to deliver simple, secure, and sustainable payment and mobility solutions enabling reach to the last mile.
